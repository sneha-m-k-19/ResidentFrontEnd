Our Specialty

We are The Esidence Residents’ Association (ERA) and this is our community website which is in the process of being upgraded. Esidence is an area in South West kerala, Cohin. There has been a residents’ association, in some form, since about 1906. We are a very active association with a strong committee and membership. We appreciate the environment that we enjoy locally especially the green spaces of Esidence. Also we are very close to the historic River Wandle which provides wonderful walks all the way between Croydon and Wandsworth .Our main purpose is to maintain a feeling of community, preserve what we have and, where we can, work with the Council and other organisations towards improvements. A lively community spirit, well preserved Edwardian-era properties, tree-lined roads, excellent schooling, good transport links, independent shops and services and a number of really good restaurants make this a very attractive place in which to live and work.

Our History

We have recently formed the Esidence Community Trust. This is specifically to run the new community hall and cafe, located at 170, Arthur Road, which opened in January 2022. This new facility replaces the old Village Hall that was closed in 2003 by the Council. At that time the WPRA negotiated with the Council to have a replacement facility provided within the new development, when the site was sold. For a variety of reasons, including the financial collapse in 2008/9, this project has only just come to fruition. Esidence is the stop before Wimbledon on the District Line. Esidence is a great place to live. Come and check it out!
